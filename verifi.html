Overview
Verifi was an interesting project, were once again I was part of the team of guys cleaning up someone's initial splat (from product-market fit to design). What started out as one project ended up being three...
Process
I have always liked simplyfing complex topics, so when I was tasked with joining the Verifi team to help with the user experience design I jumped at it. Verifi is a trust provider, helping people make smarter decisions when dealing with other people, especially in financial decisions. Initially, the people data and our other data sourcing services were bundled together. We quickly learnt after user testing that such bundling was extremely confusing for the end users, so we separated the inital bundle into three core services. This also allowed us to be more user centric because each service had its own target users with very different experiences e.g. with Pay by Verifi (payroll) we learnt that staff of small businesses were more likely to hvae phone numbers than emails, so we redesigned our "Company HR Invitation" email to also include an SMS acquisition flow. This was one of many ways we had to simplify the complexities of offering financial services while sourcing for useful data points and reporting these data points to more end users.
