Sangalo Football Social Network
Website
Role
Lead Designer, Product Manager (Chief Operating Officer)
Overview
Sangalo is a social app for sports fans. If you love the roar of the crowd after your team scores, the sound of fans singing their hearts out, watching the match in your favourite pub, talking about football, love your team; then you are going to love Sangalo!
Problem
There is a huge disconnect between the different apects of sports- participation, community and news in Nigeria. Football (soccer to Americans) is HUUGGGEEE in Nigeria. It is great for bonding, so much so that most Nigerians watch football games in groups. However, not being able to be part of a community to share your opinions, to be part of something larger has always been a problem for football fans in Nigeria. This was also another project were I had to clean up someone else's splat. This time, it was some development team in South Africa. The UX was not applicable for the target market (e.g. iPhone designs for an Android market), the project had been overdue for over 6 months etc.
Process
First of all (after hiring a team around me), we did a full product review (UX, UI, Product-Market fit, Software, Traction). Due to the time and financial constraints placed on us by the investor, we decided against rebuilding from scratch, instead opting to clean up the UX for the current version and rebuild from scratch for the next version. We had identified a series of issues with our current product, and had to identify what could be fixed, what could be built around and what would have to be deactivated until the next version.
The Result?
Because of the competive slashes to data prices, the rising mobile content consumption figures, our own focus on mobile and our increasing traction, we were acquired by iConcepts.
